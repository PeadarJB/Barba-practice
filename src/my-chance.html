My-Chance is a DSE (decentralised social enterprise). A blockchain protocol that enables users to win prizes through saving whilst furthering their social and environmental impact. The goal of this project was to redesign the website, product and brand identity.

My-Chance is the latest progression of Prize Bond systems. A savings protocol that marries the latest technological developments in decentralised networking to allow you to maximise the impact and potential of your savings.
